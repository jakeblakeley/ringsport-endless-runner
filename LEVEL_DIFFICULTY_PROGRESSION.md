## Design Principles Applied

### 1. **Smooth Progression**
- Each level increments difficulty gradually
- No sudden difficulty spikes
- Early levels emphasize learning, later levels test mastery

### 2. **Multiple Difficulty Dimensions**
Difficulty increases through:
- **Speed**: 1.0x → 1.8x (capped at 30 units/sec for fairness)
- **Spacing**: 12-20 → 5-11 units
- **Danger**: 20% → 60% avoid obstacles
- **Pattern Difficulty**: 1-3 → 7-10 rating
- **Pattern Usage**: 50% → 85% (more crafted, less random)

### 3. **Fairness Guarantees**
- **Max speed cap at 30 units/sec** prevents impossible reaction times
- Even at 1.8x multiplier, speed is capped (10 base × 1.5 sprint × 1.8 level = 27, safe)
- At minimum spacing (5 units) and max speed (30 u/s) = 0.167s reaction time (humanly possible)

### 4. **Reward Scaling**
- Mega collectibles increase from 5% → 12% (harder levels = more rewards)
- Collectible above obstacle increases (encourages risk-taking)

### 5. **Pattern Integration**
- Early levels use 50-60% patterns (learning phase)
- Mid levels use 70-75% patterns (standard mix)
- Late levels use 80-85% patterns (mastery of known sequences)

## How to Apply These Settings

### In Unity Editor:

1. **Locate Level Config Files**:
   - `Assets/Levels/Level1 - Brevet.asset`
   - `Assets/Levels/Level2 - Ring 1 Leg 1.asset`
   - etc.

2. **For Each Level**:
   - Select the asset in Project window
   - In Inspector, apply settings from this guide
   - Verify settings with OnValidate (automatic)

3. **Quick Reference**:
   - Copy values directly from tables above
   - Settings are organized by header in Inspector
   - All values auto-validate on change

### Testing Recommendations:

1. **Playtest Each Level**:
   - Level 1 should feel tutorial-easy
   - Level 8 should feel challenging but fair
   - No level should feel impossible

2. **Check Flow**:
   - Verify no sudden difficulty spikes between levels
   - Ensure patterns match expected difficulty range

3. **Validate Fairness**:
   - Check console for speed capping messages
   - Verify no "impossible pattern" warnings
   - Test recovery zones after palisades

## Advanced Tuning Tips

### If a Level Feels Too Hard:
1. Increase min/max obstacle spacing by 1-2 units
2. Decrease avoid obstacle probability by 5-10%
3. Lower max pattern difficulty by 1
4. Increase max effective speed by 2 units/sec

### If a Level Feels Too Easy:
1. Decrease min obstacle spacing by 1 unit
2. Increase pattern usage ratio by 5-10%
3. Raise min pattern difficulty by 1
4. Increase avoid obstacle probability by 5%

### For Specific Player Feedback:
- **"Too random/unpredictable"**: Increase pattern usage ratio
- **"Too repetitive"**: Decrease pattern usage ratio, widen pattern difficulty range
- **"Too fast"**: Lower speed multiplier or max effective speed
- **"Unfair hits"**: Increase min obstacle spacing, check recovery zones
- **"Not enough coins"**: Increase collectible spawn rate, lower spacing

## Summary

This progression creates a **fair, smooth, rewarding** difficulty curve that:
- ✅ Teaches mechanics in early levels
- ✅ Gradually increases challenge across all dimensions
- ✅ Caps speed to prevent unfair situations
- ✅ Uses pattern-based generation for memorable gameplay
- ✅ Rewards skilled play with more mega collectibles
- ✅ Ensures all levels are completable with practice

The fairness improvements (solvability checks, recovery zones, speed caps, pattern validation) work together with this progression to create a polished, production-ready experience.
